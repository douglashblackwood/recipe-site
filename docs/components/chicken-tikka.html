Chicken Tikka

The house pre-cooked chicken for every curry, marinated overnight and blasted in the pizza oven. Also a standalone starter.

Marinateovernight, ideally 24 h
Cookminutes, very hot
Makes1.5 kg (≈3 curries)
SourceAl's Kitchen, house version

Ingredients

Method

  1. Mix everything and massage into the chicken. Cover and refrigerate overnight, ideally 24 hours.
  2. Skewer, and raise the skewers off the pizza-oven deck (across a tray or rack) so heat circulates.
  3. Blast and pull: very hot, short time, turning once or twice. Char outside, juicy inside; lingering "to cook through" dries it out.
  4. Keep the blackened edges; that char is the flavour the sauce can't fake.
  5. For curries, pull slightly underdone; it finishes in the sauce.
Used by: every BIR curry that says "pre-cooked chicken", the Jalfrezi and Saag Balti included. Freezes in curry-sized 550 g portions. Source: Al's Kitchen (YouTube).
