BIR Mix Powder

The house Indian curry blend. One jar does ~80% of the spicing in every BIR curry; fried early (building), unlike garam masala (finishing, off the heat).

Makesone jar
Keeps~6 weeks airtight
Used byall BIR curries

Ingredients (in tablespoons)

Method

  1. Stir together, jar airtight, keep away from light.
  2. Grind your own cumin and coriander seed if you can, but do not toast them; toasting shortens shelf life.
  3. Discard when it smells stale or sweet rather than pungent (~6 weeks).
Not to be confused with the Homemade Chilli Powder (the Mexican blend for chili and fajitas). This jar is for Indian cooking; the house chilli for Indian dishes is Kashmiri. Full doctrine in The BIR Curry System.
