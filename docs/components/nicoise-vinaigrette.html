Niçoise Vinaigrette

Anchovy-Dijon vinaigrette: the best thing in the fridge for two weeks after you make it.

Time5 min
Keeps~2 weeks, fridge
Used byUn-Composed Niçoise
SourceSerious Eats

Ingredients

Method

  1. Combine everything except the oil.
  2. Whisk in the oil slowly to emulsify, or jar everything and shake hard.
  3. Season, refrigerate; shake before each use.
Worth doubling: beyond the Niçoise, it earns its keep on blanched green beans, boiled potatoes, tinned fish and any leaves that need persuading.
