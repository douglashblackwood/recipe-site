Chicken Tikka Saag Balti

The house BIR version: tempered cumin, tikka and spinach off the shared base, with the mango chutney lift that makes this one special.

Prep15 min (base + tikka made ahead)
Cook30 min
Serves4
SourceMatt Hudson / house BIR system

Ingredients

Method

  1. Temper the cumin seeds in the hot ghee/oil until fragrant.
  2. Add the purée and fry, medium-high, 10-15 minutes, to deep amber with the oil splitting.
  3. Garlic-ginger paste, diced garlic and red pepper; fry 1-2 minutes.
  4. Mix powder, Kashmiri chilli and tomato purée; fry hard 60-90 seconds.
  5. Pour in the hot onion water with the quartered tomato and green chillies; stir smooth.
  6. Add the tikka and salt; simmer hard, lid off, 5 minutes.
  7. Meanwhile, defrost the frozen spinach and squeeze it dry, then cook it down in a little oil in a separate pan for 2-3 minutes to drive off the water.
  8. Stir in the mango chutney, the balti lift.
  9. Fold in the cooked-down spinach and warm through, 2-3 minutes, until incorporated.
  10. Off the heat: garam masala, methi, coriander. Garnish with mild red chilli.
