Chicken Tikka Saag Balti

Pre-cooked tikka and spinach through a fast balti off base gravy, with a mango chutney lift.

Prep30 min
Cook50 min
SourceMatt Hudson

Ingredients

Method

  1. Heat 2 tbsp ghee or oil in a pan over medium heat.
  2. Add the diced onion and red pepper. Fry 5-10 minutes until soft.
  3. Add the garlic-ginger paste and diced garlic cloves. Stir and fry for 1 minute.
  4. Add the spices (basaar mix, curry powder, cumin, coriander, turmeric, chilli powder) and stir continuously for 1 minute so nothing burns.
  5. Add the tomato purée and fry for another minute.
  6. Pour in half the base gravy (150 ml). Raise the heat slightly and let it caramelise and reduce.
  7. Add the chicken tikka and the rest of the base gravy. Stir well and simmer for 5 minutes.
  8. Add the mango chutney and stir to combine.
  9. Add the frozen spinach and cook 5-7 minutes until fully incorporated and hot.
  10. Sprinkle in the garam masala and salt to taste. Stir well.
  11. Garnish with coriander and mild red chilli slices. Serve hot.
House note: base gravy and pre-cooked tikka both come from The BIR Curry System.
