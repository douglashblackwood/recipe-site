The Best Vegan Chili

Rainbow Plant Life's deep, smoky two-bean chili: wine-deglazed, cocoa-backed, 90-minute simmer.

Prep~20 min
Cook2 h
SourceRainbow Plant Life

Ingredients

Method

  1. Heat the oil over medium-high. Cook the onions with a pinch of salt until golden, ~10 minutes.
  2. Add garlic, jalapeños and tomato paste; 2-3 minutes until the paste darkens.
  3. Stir in chilli powder, cumin, smoked paprika and oregano; 30 seconds, stirring constantly.
  4. Deglaze with the red wine, scraping up the fond. Simmer a minute.
  5. Add broth, both beans, chipotles with adobo, bay leaves, cocoa, tamari and crushed tomatoes.
  6. Season with salt and pepper. Boil, then drop to a gentle simmer.
  7. Simmer 1½ hours, stirring every 10 minutes, until thick and velvety.
  8. Stir in maple syrup, lime juice and red wine vinegar. Adjust seasoning.
  9. Fish out the bay leaves and serve.

Source: Rainbow Plant Life
