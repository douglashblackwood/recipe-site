Turkey and Courgette Burgers

Ottolenghi's juicy little burgers: grated courgette and herbs keep the turkey light. Makes ~18.

Prep15 min
Cook35 min
Makes~18
SourceYotam Ottolenghi

Ingredients

  • Burgers
  • Sumac yoghurt sauce

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 220°C / 200°C fan.
  2. Mix the burger ingredients (everything bar the sauce and the oil) in a large bowl until evenly combined.
  3. Shape into burgers of about 45 g each (roughly 18).
  4. Heat a thin layer of olive oil (~2 mm) in a frying pan. Sear in batches over medium heat, ~4 minutes, turning until golden.
  5. Transfer to a lined oven tray and bake 5-7 minutes until cooked through. Serve warm or at room temperature.
  6. Sumac yoghurt sauce: stir the Greek yoghurt with the lemon zest and juice, grated garlic, sumac, olive oil and salt. Serve alongside the burgers.
