Pasta Alla Norma

Ottolenghi's Sicilian classic: roasted aubergine, tomato, pecorino and basil.

Prep45 min
Cook60 min
SourceYotam Ottolenghi

Ingredients

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 230°C.
  2. Peel the aubergine in stripes, cut into 1 cm slices. Salt the slices and leave 20-30 minutes, then pat dry.
  3. Toss the aubergine with 75 ml oil, salt and pepper. Roast 30-35 minutes until golden.
  4. Heat 30 ml oil, sauté the garlic and chillies 1-2 minutes. Add the tomatoes, oregano, sugar, salt and pepper. Cook 10 minutes until thick.
  5. Cook the spaghetti al dente. Mix with the sauce, the roasted aubergine, two-thirds of the pecorino and the basil. Loosen with pasta water if needed.
  6. Divide into bowls, top with the remaining pecorino, basil and the last 15 ml of oil.
Data note: the tomatoes appear in the method but were missing from the source file's ingredient list; a 400 g tin has been added here as the sensible quantity.
