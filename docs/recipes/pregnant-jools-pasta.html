Pregnant Jools Pasta

Jamie's sausage penne: blitzed veg and sausage meat with fennel seeds in a balsamic-tomato sauce.

Prep20 min
Cook30 min
Serves4
SourceJamie Oliver

Ingredients

Method

  1. Trim the spring onions, carrot and celery. Roughly chop, then blitz in the food processor with the chillies (stalks removed).
  2. Add the sausages, fennel seeds and oregano. Pulse until well mixed.
  3. Spoon the mixture into a hot frying pan with a lug of olive oil. Break it up and stir while it browns.
  4. Cook the penne in a large pan of boiling salted water according to the packet.
  5. Crush the garlic into the sausage pan, then stir in the balsamic vinegar and tinned tomatoes. Season and simmer until the pasta is ready.
  6. Drain the penne, reserving a cup of cooking water. Toss the pasta through the sauce, loosening with pasta water as needed.
  7. Tear over the basil and serve, with Parmesan if you like.
