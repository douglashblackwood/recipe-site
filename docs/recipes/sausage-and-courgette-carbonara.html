Sausage and Courgette Carbonara

Sausage balls and courgette through linguine in a lemony yolk-and-parmesan sauce, stabilised with a cornflour slurry.

Prep20 min
Cook30 min
Serves2-3
SourceJamie Oliver (adapted)

Ingredients

Method

  1. Cook the linguine in salted water per the packet. Prepare everything else while it cooks.
  2. Trim the courgettes, quarter lengthways, cut into small chunks.
  3. Heat a large skillet, medium, with the olive oil. Skin the sausages and roll the meat into small balls (~2.5 cm).
  4. Brown the sausage balls 5-6 minutes. Add the courgette and cook until softened. Turn off the heat.
  5. Whisk the yolks, lemon zest and half the parmesan in a mixing bowl. Separately, mix the cornflour with the water.
  6. Tong the hot linguine straight into the yolk bowl; the residual heat thickens the eggs without scrambling.
  7. Add the cornflour slurry to the skillet and stir over low heat until it thickens.
  8. Combine pasta and sauce in the skillet, tossing well. Loosen with pasta water if needed; season.
  9. Serve with the remaining parmesan.
