Macaroni Cheese

Classic roux-based mac and cheese with a grilled cheddar top.

SourceHome recipe
StatusRetired

Ingredients

Method

  1. Cook the macaroni according to the packet. Drain and set aside.
  2. Melt the butter in a large saucepan over medium heat.
  3. Add the flour and stir continuously for 1-2 minutes to make a roux.
  4. Gradually add the milk, whisking constantly to prevent lumps.
  5. Keep stirring until the sauce thickens.
  6. Off the heat, stir in most of the cheddar, reserving some for the top.
  7. Stir the macaroni through the cheese sauce.
  8. Transfer to a baking dish, sprinkle the remaining cheese on top.
  9. Optional: grill or bake until golden and bubbly.
  10. Serve hot.
Retired: incompatible with the house no-heavy-dairy rule. Kept for the record (7 historical cooks).
