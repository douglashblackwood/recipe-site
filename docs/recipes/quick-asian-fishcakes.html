Quick Asian Fishcakes

Jamie's 5-ingredient salmon patties with lemongrass, ginger and a chilli jam glaze.

Prep12 min
Cook22 min
Serves4
SourceJamie Oliver

Ingredients

Method

  1. Whack the lemongrass against the work surface and remove the tough outer layer.
  2. Peel the ginger, then very finely chop with the lemongrass core and most of the coriander, stalks and all, reserving a few nice leaves in cold water.
  3. Chop the salmon into 1 cm chunks over the mix. Push half aside; chop the rest almost to a purée, then mix the chunky bits back through. Season with sea salt and black pepper.
  4. Divide into 4, shape and squash into 2 cm-thick patties.
  5. Cook in a large non-stick pan, medium-high, 1 tbsp olive oil: 2 minutes per side until golden.
  6. Spoon over the chilli jam, add a splash of water, turn the heat off and jiggle the pan to glaze.
  7. Serve sprinkled with the drained coriander leaves.

Source: Jamie Oliver, 5 Ingredients
