Shawarma Tahini

Brian Lagerstrom's big salad: shawarma-spiced charred chicken, roasted cauliflower and sweet potato, pickled raisins, pistachios and a creamy tahini dressing.

Prep30 min
Cook60 min
Serves4
SourceBrian Lagerstrom

Ingredients

  • Pickled raisins
  • Roast veg
  • Chicken
  • Salad
  • Tahini dressing

Method

  1. Heat the sugar and vinegar to a simmer, add the raisins, stir and take off the heat. Plump for 20 minutes.
  2. Preheat a sheet tray in a 230°C oven. Toss the cauliflower and sweet potato with salt and a long squeeze of olive oil; spread on the hot tray and roast 15 minutes.
  3. Coat the chicken in salt, pepper, oregano, garam masala, garlic powder, poultry seasoning, cumin, yoghurt and lemon juice. Lay on a foil-lined tray.
  4. Bake the chicken 10 minutes at 230°C, then char under the grill on high for 3-5 minutes.
  5. Dressing: stir tahini, salt, lemon juice, vinegar, honey, garlic, olive oil and water until creamy.
  6. Assemble: rocket and roasted veg, then the chicken sliced over, drained pickled raisins, cucumber and pistachios scattered, and a generous drizzle of tahini dressing.
