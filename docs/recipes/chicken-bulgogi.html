Chicken Bulgogi (Dak Bulgogi)

The gateway Korean: soy-honey marinated chicken thighs, not spicy by default, gochujang on the side for the adults.

Marinate2-12 h
Cook15 min
Serves2 (double it)
SourceKimchimari
StatusTrial

Ingredients

  • Marinade
  • To serve

Method

  1. Trim the thighs and flatten the thicker sections so they cook evenly.
  2. Mix the marinade, stirring the grated onion and its juice in with the garlic, ginger and sesame oil.
  3. Marinate the chicken 2-12 hours, reserving a little marinade.
  4. Medium heat, lightly oiled pan: 2-3 minutes per side until cooked through. Medium, not high; the sugars burn before the inside cooks. (Or pizza-oven it on a tray for char.)
  5. Reduce the reserved marinade in the hot pan to a glaze and toss the cooked chicken through it.
  6. Rest briefly, slice, serve over rice with spring onion; gochujang on the side.
Trial notes: deliberately mild, this is the kids' introduction to Korean food. The adult upgrade is a spoon of gochujang thinned with a little of the pan juices.
