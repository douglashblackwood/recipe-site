Un-Composed Niçoise Salad

Serious Eats' tossed (not plated) Niçoise: anchovy vinaigrette through potatoes, beans, tuna, olives and eggs.

Prep30 min
Cook~1 h
SourceSerious Eats

Ingredients

  • Vinaigrette
  • Salad

Method

  1. Vinaigrette: combine shallot, garlic, Dijon, anchovies, vinegar and water; slowly whisk in the olive oil to emulsify (or shake everything in a jar). Season. Keeps refrigerated up to 2 weeks.
  2. Potatoes: cover with 5 cm of water, add onion, thyme, garlic and a generous handful of salt. Simmer until fork-tender, ~40 minutes. Drain, cool slightly, crumble.
  3. Beans: blanch in boiling salted water ~3 minutes until tender-crisp, then straight into iced water. Drain, pat dry, cut into 4 cm pieces.
  4. Eggs: steam over 2 cm of boiling water for 9 minutes, then ice bath for 15. Peel and quarter.
  5. Toss potatoes, beans, tomatoes, anchovies, tuna, olives, capers, basil and greens with the vinaigrette.
  6. Serve garnished with the quartered eggs, salt and freshly ground pepper.
