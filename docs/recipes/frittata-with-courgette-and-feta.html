Frittata with Courgette and Feta

Stovetop-to-grill frittata: set on the hob, finished under the grill. One pan, on the table in 20 minutes.

Prep5 min
Cook15 min
Serves4
SourceRecipeTin Eats

Ingredients

Method

  1. Preheat the grill to high.
  2. Whisk eggs and milk with a pinch of salt and pepper. Set aside.
  3. Heat the olive oil in an oven-safe, non-stick frying pan (24-26 cm) over medium-high heat.
  4. Cook the courgette 3-4 minutes until lightly golden. Add spring onions and garlic, 1 minute more.
  5. Season the vegetables and spread them evenly across the pan.
  6. Pour in the egg mixture, scatter the feta over. Cook on the hob 3-4 minutes until the edges set but the centre still wobbles.
  7. Transfer to the grill for 3-4 minutes until golden on top and just set in the centre.
  8. Rest 2 minutes, then cut into wedges and serve from the pan.
Notes: no cream, which keeps it inside the house no-heavy-dairy rule (feta is a light finish, not a creamy base). Any firm vegetable works: cherry tomatoes, peppers, peas, spinach. Don't overcook under the grill, the eggs keep setting off the heat. Good cold the next day.
