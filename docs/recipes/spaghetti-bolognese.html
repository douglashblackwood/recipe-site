Spaghetti Bolognese

House bolognese: soffritto base, slow 40-minute simmer, finished with parmesan.

Prep15 min
Cook45 min
Serves4
SourceHouse recipe

Ingredients

Method

  1. Heat the oil in a large pan or casserole over medium heat.
  2. Add the onion, carrot and celery (the soffritto). Cook gently 8-10 minutes until soft.
  3. Add the garlic; 1 minute until fragrant.
  4. Up to medium-high, add the mince, break it up and brown all over, 5-7 minutes.
  5. Stir in the tomato purée, 1-2 minutes.
  6. Add the tinned tomatoes (breaking them up), stock and wine if using.
  7. Add oregano and bay leaf, season.
  8. Simmer low, partially covered, 30-40 minutes, stirring occasionally, until thick.
  9. Cook the spaghetti al dente towards the end.
  10. Drain, reserving a cup of pasta water. Remove the bay leaf.
  11. Toss the spaghetti through the sauce, loosening with pasta water as needed.
  12. Serve with freshly grated parmesan.
