Turkey Meatballs

Gordon Ramsay's lean turkey meatballs in a balsamic tomato sauce over linguine.

Prep30 min (incl. chilling)
Cook60 min
Serves4
SourceGordon Ramsay

Ingredients

  • Meatballs
  • Sauce & pasta

Method

  1. Mix the turkey mince with the finely chopped onion, garlic, Worcestershire sauce, beaten egg, salt and pepper.
  2. With wet hands, roll into 20 meatballs. Cover and chill 30 minutes.
  3. Sauce: heat olive oil over medium, soften the diced onion 5-6 minutes, garlic for 1 more.
  4. Add the tomato purée, 2 minutes, then tomatoes, oregano, balsamic, salt and pepper. Simmer 10 minutes.
  5. Brown the meatballs in batches in olive oil, then transfer to the sauce and cook 10 minutes, adding 50-100 ml water if it gets too thick.
  6. Cook the linguine al dente; drain and toss with a teaspoon of oil.
  7. Linguine into bowls, meatballs and sauce on top, parmesan if you like.

Source: Gordon Ramsay
