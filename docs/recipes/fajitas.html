Fajitas

House freestyle: chicken, peppers and onions, seasoned and seared, wrapped at the table. Quick, kid-approved, no written recipe.

SourcePersonal creation

How it goes

This one is cooked from memory rather than a recipe: sliced chicken breast and strips of pepper and onion, tossed in fajita seasoning, seared hot in a wide pan, then served with warm tortillas, and whatever's in the fridge: soured-cream alternative, salsa, grated cheese, lime.

If a definitive written version ever settles, it belongs here.
