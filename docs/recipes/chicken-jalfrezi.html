Chicken Jalfrezi

The house BIR version: charred peppers, onions and green chillies through a hot, dry, clinging sauce off the shared base. Signatures from Al's Kitchen kept.

Prep15 min (base + tikka made ahead)
Cook30 min
Serves4
SourceAl's Kitchen / house BIR system

Ingredients

Method

  1. Char the pepper and onion chunks hard (grill or pizza oven); set aside.
  2. Fry the purée in the oil, medium-high, 10-15 minutes, to deep amber with the oil splitting out.
  3. Garlic-ginger paste, 1 minute. Then mix powder, tandoori masala, Kashmiri chilli and the loosened tomato purée; fry hard 60-90 seconds.
  4. Add the Naga (if using) and green chillies. Pour in the hot onion water; stir smooth.
  5. Add the tikka, salt and sugar. Boil hard, lid off, 5-8 minutes; jalfrezi should be thick and clinging.
  6. Fold in the charred peppers and onion near the end so they keep their bite.
  7. Off the heat: garam masala, methi crushed in the palms, coriander.
House note: converged onto The BIR Curry System on 11/06/2026. The old standalone version (blended boiled onions, raw chicken, 150 ml oil) is retired.
