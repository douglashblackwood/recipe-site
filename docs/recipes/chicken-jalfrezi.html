Chicken Jalfrezi

Al's Kitchen full version: charred peppers, onions and green chillies through a hot, reduced sauce.

Prep30 min
Cook50 min
SourceAl's Kitchen

Ingredients

Method

  1. Heat 150 ml olive oil in a large pan over medium heat.
  2. Add the blended boiled onions and cook for 8 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  3. Add the star anise, cinnamon stick, garlic-ginger paste, and a pinch each of salt and sugar. Cook for 2 more minutes.
  4. Add the dry spices: curry powder, garam masala, turmeric, tandoori masala, Kashmiri chilli powder and kasoori methi. Stir well to bloom the spices.
  5. Add the tomato purée mixture and the Naga pickle (if using). Combine thoroughly.
  6. Stir in the chicken, coating it well in the spice mixture.
  7. Pour in the 500 ml reserved onion water. Cover and simmer for 10 minutes.
  8. Add the charred onions, peppers and green chillies. Stir to incorporate.
  9. Lid off, cook until the sauce reduces and thickens to your liking. Adjust salt and sugar.
  10. Stir in chopped coriander just before serving. Optional: a teaspoon of soy sauce for extra depth.
House note: this is Al's standalone version with raw chicken cooked in the sauce. For the systematised cook off the shared base and pre-cooked tikka, see the Jalfrezi entry in The BIR Curry System.
