Vegan Red Lentil Curry

Rainbow Plant Life's creamy one-pot lentil curry: coconut milk and almond butter give it body, lemon lifts it.

Prep35 min
Cook45 min
Serves4
SourceRainbow Plant Life

Ingredients

Method

  1. Rinse the lentils in cold water until it runs clear.
  2. Heat the coconut oil in a large deep pan, medium-high. Garlic, ginger, turmeric and chilli for 2 minutes, stirring often.
  3. Add cumin, coriander, chilli powder, curry powder, garam masala, salt and pepper; 30-60 seconds until fragrant.
  4. Add the broth, scraping up the browned bits, then the lentils and tomatoes. Low heat, covered, 20-25 minutes.
  5. Lid off; stir in coconut milk and almond butter. Season; cook uncovered 5-8 minutes until thick.
  6. Stir in lemon juice and coriander. Optional: a quick blitz with the stick blender for extra creaminess.
  7. Serve with rice and/or flatbread, more coriander on top.
House note: this is a traditional one-pot, deliberately separate from The BIR Curry System; the BIR lentil slot is the dhansak.
