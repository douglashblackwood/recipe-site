Grilled Salmon with Lemongrass and Soy

Marinated in lemongrass, ginger and soy, brushed with honey and grilled dark and sticky.

Prep10 min + 1 h marinade
Cook10 min
SourceJamie Oliver

Ingredients

Method

  1. Bash up the lemongrass. Pick the coriander leaves and finely slice the stalks. Peel and finely grate the ginger and garlic.
  2. Mix lemongrass, coriander stalks, ginger and garlic with the soy sauce. Rub all over the salmon and marinate for 1 hour (bag or covered tray).
  3. Grill on the highest setting: remove the salmon from the marinade, brush with honey, grill 10 minutes.
  4. Deseed and finely slice the chilli with the spring onions.
  5. Fork up the cooked salmon to expose the dark, sticky outside and the juicy pink fish underneath.
  6. Scatter over chilli, spring onions and the reserved coriander leaves. Squeeze over the lime to serve.

Source: Jamie Oliver
