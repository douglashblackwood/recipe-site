Chicken Pho (Pho Ga)

Spiced chicken broth, rice noodles, and a table full of toppings. The Ninja's pressure mode turns the 90-minute simmer into a hands-off half hour.

Prep10 min
Cook1½ h hob, or ~30 min pressure
Serves4-6
SourceRecipeTin Eats
StatusTrial

Ingredients

  • Broth
  • Bowls & toppings

Method

  1. Char the onion, ginger and shallots HARD and dry (no oil) until dark brown, ideally in the pizza oven, or on a hot dry pan / under the grill. (Ninja: sear mode, dry.)
  2. Add water and all broth ingredients (including the coriander stems) except the salt. Hob: simmer gently 1½ hours, lid ajar, skimming. Ninja: pressure cook 30 minutes, natural release.
  3. Strain into a clean pot (~1.5 litres), add the salt, return to a gentle simmer. Shred the chicken meat.
  4. Cook the noodles per the packet just before serving.
  5. Noodles into bowls, chicken on top, ~375 ml hot broth over, spring onion sprinkled.
  6. Toppings on the table; everyone dresses their own.
Trial notes: kids get the mild base bowl (noodles, chicken, clear broth, hoisin if they like); the chilli, herbs and lime live on the table. Broth freezes well, so a double batch banks two future dinners.
