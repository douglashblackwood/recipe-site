Pizza

House dough: Tipo 00 with diastatic malt, autolysed, KitchenAid-kneaded, rested 4-5 hours. Makes 4 pizzas.

Prep15-20 min
Prove + cook~5½ h
Makes4 pizzas
SourceHouse recipe

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Topping

Method

  1. Autolyse: mix the flour, malt powder and 225 ml warm water until just combined. Rest 20-30 minutes.
  2. Activate the yeast: mix yeast, sugar and 50 ml warm water (27-29°C). Leave 5 minutes until frothy.
  3. Add the salt to the autolysed flour mixture.
  4. With the dough hook, add the yeast mixture (and optional olive oil) to the dough. Mix on speed 2 until combined.
  5. Knead on speed 4 for 5-7 minutes until smooth and elastic.
  6. Cover the bowl and rest the dough 20 minutes at room temperature.
  7. Divide into 4 balls of about 180-200 g.
  8. Rest the balls, covered, at room temperature for 4-5 hours.
  9. Preheat the oven and pizza stone to maximum (~230°C+).
  10. Stretch a ball into a base and par-bake on the stone for 3-5 minutes until slightly firm.
  11. Spread ~75 g sauce and top with ~80 g mozzarella.
  12. Bake 7-10 minutes until the crust is golden and the cheese bubbles.
  13. Optional: 1-2 minutes under the grill for char.
  14. Rest 2-3 minutes to set the cheese before slicing.
  15. Finish with a drizzle of extra virgin olive oil and fresh herbs if you like.
