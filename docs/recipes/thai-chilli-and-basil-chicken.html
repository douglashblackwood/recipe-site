Thai Chilli and Basil Chicken

Gordon Ramsay's hot wok stir-fry: bird's-eye chillies, oyster-fish-soy sauce, two basils, jasmine rice.

Prep25 min
Cook45 min
Serves4
SourceGordon Ramsay

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Stir-fry
  • Sauce
  • Finish

Method

  1. Wash the rice three times until the water runs clear. Rice, measured water, pinch of salt; boil, then low simmer with the lid on for 10-12 minutes until absorbed.
  2. Prep the chicken and vegetables. Season the chicken with salt and white pepper.
  3. Wok on high. 1 tbsp oil; stir-fry the chicken in quarters, 1 minute each until lightly browned, removing each batch.
  4. Mix oyster sauce, soy, stock, fish sauce and sugar in one bowl; cornflour and water in another.
  5. More oil, then garlic and half the chillies for 1 minute.
  6. Onion 2 minutes, then broccoli and beans 2 more, splashing in water if it sticks.
  7. Chicken back in, 2-3 minutes.
  8. Sauce in, then the cornflour paste. Thai basil in for a final minute.
  9. Rice into bowls, stir-fry on top, garnish with the remaining chillies and basil.

Source: Gordon Ramsay, Quick & Delicious
