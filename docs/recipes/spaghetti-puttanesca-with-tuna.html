Spaghetti Puttanesca with Tuna

Punchy storecupboard pasta: olives, capers, anchovy and chilli with flaked tuna. Deliberately bold; not one for the kids.

Serves4
SourceHouse recipe

Ingredients

Method

  1. Pasta: boil in well-salted water until al dente.
  2. Sauce: meanwhile, heat 3 tbsp olive oil over medium. Garlic and chilli in, 1-2 minutes until fragrant, not browned.
  3. Aromatics: add the anchovies and let them dissolve into the oil. Capers in for another minute.
  4. Build: add tomatoes and olives. Simmer 8-10 minutes until slightly thickened. Season.
  5. Tuna: flake in and fold gently; heat through 2-3 minutes.
  6. Combine: reserve a cup of pasta water, drain, toss the spaghetti through the sauce, loosening as needed.
  7. Finish: scatter with parsley and serve with extra black pepper.
Notes: easy cook, one-pan sauce. For a milder version reduce or omit the chilli and anchovies. Any long pasta works.
