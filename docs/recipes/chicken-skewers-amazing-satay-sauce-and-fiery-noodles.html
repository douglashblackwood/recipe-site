Chicken Skewers, Amazing Satay Sauce & Fiery Noodles

Jamie's 30 Minute Meals: grilled satay chicken kebabs with honeyed cashew noodles.

Prep20 min
Cook35 min
Serves4
SourceJamie Oliver

Ingredients

Method

  1. Get all ingredients and equipment ready. Grill on full heat. Soak 4 wooden skewers in cold water.
  2. Satay sauce: blitz coriander, chilli, garlic, peanut butter, soy sauce, ginger, lime zest and juice in a food processor. Add water to a spoonable paste. Season; set half aside for serving.
  3. Thread the chicken onto skewers to make 4 kebabs. Lightly score both sides.
  4. Toss the skewers in the remaining satay sauce, rubbing it into the meat. Drizzle with olive oil, season with salt.
  5. Grill 8-10 minutes per side until golden and cooked through, drizzling with honey near the end.
  6. Noodles: soak the nests in boiling water for 6 minutes. Toast the cashews in a pan until golden, then toss with honey.
  7. Blitz red onion, chilli and coriander stalks. Mix with soy sauce, sesame oil, fish sauce, lime juice and olive oil in a serving bowl.
  8. Drain and refresh the noodles, toss with the dressing, add cashews and coriander leaves.
  9. Serve the skewers with the noodles and reserved satay sauce on the side.

Source: Jamie Oliver, 30 Minute Meals
