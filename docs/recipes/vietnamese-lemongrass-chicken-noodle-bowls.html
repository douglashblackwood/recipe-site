Vietnamese Lemongrass Chicken Noodle Bowls

Bun Ga Nuong: build-your-own vermicelli bowls with lemongrass chicken and nuoc cham. Cousin of the house lemongrass salmon, fajitas-style assembly.

Prep20 min + 1-24 h marinade
Cook10 min
Serves4
SourceRecipeTin Eats
StatusTrial

Ingredients

  • Chicken & marinade
  • Nuoc cham (serve on the side)
  • Bowls

Method

  1. Marinate the chicken with all marinade ingredients (including the oyster sauce and honey), 1-24 hours in the fridge.
  2. While the chicken marinates, quick-pickle the julienned carrot in the rice vinegar with a pinch of sugar; leave to steep, then drain before serving.
  3. Nuoc cham: combine everything, dissolve the sugar, rest 20 minutes. Taste and balance.
  4. Cook the chicken in a little oil over high heat, 6-8 minutes until golden, lacquered and cooked, turning to char and glaze. Rest 5 minutes, slice thinly, scatter with toasted sesame seeds. (Or pizza-oven it for char.)
  5. Soak the vermicelli in hot water 3 minutes, drain, rinse cold.
  6. Everyone builds their own: noodles, vegetables, herbs, chicken; nuoc cham drizzled to taste.
Trial notes: the nuoc cham carries the chilli, so the kids' bowls stay mild by default; that's the on-ramp. Same assembly theatre as fajitas, which this family demonstrably loves.
