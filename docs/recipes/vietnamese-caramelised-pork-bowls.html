Vietnamese Caramelised Pork Bowls

Sweet-salty caramelised pork mince over rice: six minutes of actual cooking. First Vietnamese trial.

Prep10 min
Cook10 min
Serves4
SourceRecipeTin Eats
StatusTrial

Ingredients

  • To serve

Method

  1. Heat the oil in a large pan over high heat.
  2. Sauté onion, ginger, garlic (and chilli, lemongrass) for 2 minutes.
  3. Add the pork and cook ~2 minutes until no longer pink, breaking it up.
  4. Add the sugar and fish sauce. Leave it without stirring until the juices cook out and it starts to caramelise, ~2 minutes.
  5. Stir, then repeat the leave-it-alone caramelisation twice more until dark and sticky in patches.
  6. Pour in the coconut water and a good grind of black pepper, scraping up the sticky bits, and let it bubble down to a glaze, ~1 minute.
  7. Serve over rice with spring onion and the fresh bits alongside.
Trial notes: the no-stirring caramelisation is the whole technique. Works with chicken or turkey mince too. Kid version is just the pot before extra chilli; the sweetness does the persuading.
