Red Curry with King Prawns

Rosa's Thai: cracked coconut cream, red curry paste, and prawns cooked separately so they stay sweet.

Prep20 min
Cook30 min
Serves2
SourceRosa's Thai Cafe

Ingredients

Method

  1. Heat the thick coconut cream (or coconut oil) in a wok over medium heat. Add the red curry paste and sauté until fragrant.
  2. Stir in 200 ml coconut milk, the fish sauce and palm sugar. Boil, then reduce to medium, stirring ~3 minutes until the oil separates and the sauce is smooth and thick.
  3. Add the remaining coconut milk (keeping 1 tbsp for garnish). Boil again, then low heat; balance spicy, salty and sweet to taste.
  4. Pat the prawns dry and pan-fry separately in a little coconut oil until pink and just cooked, 3-5 minutes.
  5. Plate the jasmine rice, add the prawns, pour over the red curry sauce.
  6. Garnish with lime leaf, chilli, galangal and Thai basil. Drizzle over the reserved coconut milk.
