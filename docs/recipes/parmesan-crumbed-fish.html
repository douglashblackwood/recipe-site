Parmesan Crumbed Fish

Emergency weeknight fish: parmesan-panko crust on Dijon-smeared fillets, hot skillet then grill. 12 minutes total.

Prep5 min
Cook7 min
Serves2
SourceRecipeTin Eats
StatusRetired

Ingredients

  • Fish
  • Parmesan crumb
  • To serve

Method

  1. Preheat the grill to high.
  2. Mix all crumb ingredients until evenly combined.
  3. Season the fish both sides. Spread Dijon on the top side only.
  4. Press the mustard side firmly into the crumb so it sticks. Spray the crumb with olive oil for colour.
  5. Heat ½ tbsp olive oil in an oven-safe skillet over high heat until very hot.
  6. Place fillets crumb-side up in the pan, then straight under the grill for 5-6 minutes until the crust is deep golden and the fish cooked through.
  7. Serve immediately with lemon wedges.
Notes: the hot skillet cooks the underside while the grill browns the crust, that's the speed trick. Oven alternative: 220°C for 10-12 minutes, then a quick grill for colour. Parmesan is a light coating only, within the house dairy tolerance. Retired 10/05/2026: the household didn't fancy it.
